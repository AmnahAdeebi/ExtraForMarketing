Nutrition Value

The ketogenic (keto) diet, characterized by high-fat, moderate-protein, and very low-carbohydrate intake, offers several health benefits. By shifting the body's metabolism to burn fat for fuel instead of carbohydrates, it can lead to significant weight loss and improved body composition. The diet also helps stabilize blood sugar levels, making it beneficial for those with type 2 diabetes or insulin resistance. Additionally, the keto diet may enhance mental clarity and focus by providing a steady energy source to the brain. Some studies suggest it can reduce inflammation and lower the risk of chronic diseases, contributing to overall health improvement.
